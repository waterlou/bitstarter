Find different to game action games on your iPhone using touch screen? BluePad help you to play classic action games on your iPhone 5 using ergometic blue booth controller. The pad come with 1 4-ways joy pad, two analog thumb pad and four buttons. It also come with a start and pause button and a signal LED. The pad connect to your iPhone 5 wirelessly using bluetooth. No driver is needed. The BluePad powered by 1000mAH built-in rechargable battery.

  • Bluetooth 2.0
  • One 4-ways joy pad, two analog thumb pad
  • Four buttons
  • One start and one pause button
  • One LED indicator
  • 1000mAH rechargable battery
  • Very light weight and easy to carry

FAQ

Will it work with Android?
The bluetooth connection should work with Android device, but the mount to iPhone 5 should not work with other models.
Will it come with different color?
In the inital launch, only black color will be produced.
